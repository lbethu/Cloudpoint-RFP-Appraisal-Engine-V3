# Compliance Matrix Template

## Purpose

The compliance matrix converts an RFP into an actionable checklist.

Instead of only summarizing the RFP, the assistant should identify what must be prepared, who should own each item, what the current status is, how risky the item is, and what notes are needed for proposal planning.

## Standard Compliance Matrix

| Requirement | Owner | Status | Risk Level | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| RFP document available | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | High | Confirm the actual solicitation document is available before appraisal |
| Client / agency name | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Needed for opportunity tracking |
| Project title | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Needed for proposal naming and tracking |
| Project location | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | High | Needed for geography and drive-time review |
| Submission deadline | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | High | Missing deadline should trigger Conditional Go until confirmed |
| Question deadline | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Needed to submit clarifying questions |
| Pre-bid meeting | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing / Not Applicable | Medium | Mandatory meetings increase risk |
| Submission method | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | High | Portal, email, or hard copy |
| Page limit | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Impacts proposal strategy |
| Required forms | Admin / Operations | Found / Missing / Needed | High | Required forms must be completed before submission |
| Insurance requirements | Admin / Operations | Found / Missing / Needs Review | High | Review limits and coverage requirements |
| Bonding requirements | Leadership | Found / Missing / Needs Review | High | Bonding may create No-Go risk |
| Security requirements | Technical Lead | Found / Missing / Needs Review | High | CJIS, background checks, cybersecurity rules, or secure hosting |
| Scope of work | Technical Lead | Found / Missing | High | Needed for capability-fit review |
| Technical approach | Technical Lead | Needed | Medium | Must align with Cloudpoint capabilities |
| Pricing / fee proposal | Leadership / Finance | Needed / Unknown | High | Confirm pricing strategy |
| References | Proposal Lead | Needed | Medium | Use relevant local government examples |
| Resumes / staff qualifications | Proposal Lead | Needed | Medium | Confirm availability and page limits |
| Work samples | Proposal Lead | Needed | Medium | Use relevant project examples |
| Field work requirement | Technical Lead | Found / Missing / Unknown | High | Check for GPS collection, field verification, inspections, asset inventory |
| Onsite meetings | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing / Unknown | Medium | Recurring onsite meetings may increase travel burden |
| Subcontractor need | Leadership | Yes / No / Possible / Unknown | High | Identify partner type if surveying, drone, LiDAR, or civil engineering appears |
| Award criteria | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Needed to understand how proposal will be judged |
| Evaluation scoring | Proposal Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Check weights for price, experience, technical approach |
| Contract terms | Leadership / Legal Review | Found / Missing / Needs Review | High | Review liability, indemnification, termination, and payment terms |
| Deliverables | Technical Lead | Found / Missing | High | Confirm deliverables are achievable |
| Schedule requirements | Technical Lead | Found / Missing | Medium | Aggressive timelines increase risk |
| Final recommendation | Leadership | Needed | High | Go, Conditional Go, or No-Go |
